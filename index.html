About me

Hi! I am Ke YAN from Beijing, China. Now I am a postdoc researcher in National Institutes of Health, US. My tutors are Dr. Ronald Summers and Dr. Le Lu. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, and machine learning. I am using deep learning methods to tackle difficult problems in medical image analysis.

I got my Ph.D. and BS degrees both from Dept. Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, China. My advisor is Prof. David Zhang, IEEE fellow. I am the winner of 2016 Tsinghua University Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award.

E-mail: yankethu <at> foxmail.com
