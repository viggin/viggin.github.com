About me

Hi! I am Ke YAN, a PhD candidate in Dept. Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua Univ., China. My advisor is Prof. David Zhang, IEEE fellow.

In my PhD work, I focus on analyzing electronic nose (e-nose) data with machine learning algorithms. For example, compensating sensor drift with transfer learning.

E-mail: yank10@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn
